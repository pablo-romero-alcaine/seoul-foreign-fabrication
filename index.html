Go-Kart

- This is the go-kart that we made for the event shown in the right

- It took us around 2 months to create the go-kart that looks like a bicycle

- The other go-kart was made last year but we upgraded it by making it look better and increasing its speed

- The maximum speed the go-kart could go was 30 kilometers per hour

- Safety is very important so we added a seatbelt as well and wore a helmet when riding

Go-Kart Event

- This is the go kart event where a lot of people created their own cars and then raced against others

- There were different rewards, one for the first car to reach the finish line, and one for the best looking car

- We made our go-kart for speed hoping that we would win the race but we came third

- This was a very fun event and it is being held every year, we would love for more people to come
